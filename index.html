JOTTO

The logic deduction game.

How to Play

  1. Think of a 5-letter word (no repeating letters).
  2. The Computer will also think of a secret word.
  3. Take turns guessing.
  4. Score 0-5 based on how many letters match (position doesn't matter).
  5. First to deduce the opponent's word wins!
